About Me

Growing up in the late 90's and early 2000's I always had some kind of technology close at hand. It started with a Gameboy color which evolved into gaming consoles such as an Xbox or PlayStation. Later I progressed to building high end desktop computers mainly for gaming and other similar uses. This love of technology and computers has gone with me wherever I go and now I hope to make a career doing something I love. While in college at Texas Tech University I studied Anthropology and Archaeology and after graduating began working to uncover the secrets of the past. I found a way to integrate technology into this as well, utilizing Geographic Information technology to preserve the sites I worked on. I persued more knowledge on Geographic Information Systems (GIS) by earning a Masters certificate from SouthWest Missouri State University. It was during this course that I found my passion for building websites and computer programs. While at SW Missouri State I attended a class for Geoprocessing using Python. I loved writing code to complete geoprocessing tasks and found I was quite good at it.

Since finishing my Masters program I have dedicated my time to learning how to create websites. I have utlized online resources such as codecademy.com to learn technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Redux and Git. I have used these resources to creates websites and web apps. My background in Anthropology makes it easy for me to create high quality sites with a high level of usability. I continue to build my knowledge base with these technologies as well as others and look forward to the day that I can turn my passion into a career.
